Question 2

What combination of OGC Service and Request (e.g. SERVICE=WMS&REQUEST=GetFeatureInfo) would you use to perform the following? (3 pts each)

Answers:

Determine the spatial extent of an available layer from a Web Map Service.

  • SERVICE=WMS & REQUEST=GetCapabilities

Obtain a list of coverages from a Web Coverage service.

  • SERVICE=WCS & REQUEST=GetCapabilities

Determine what file formats are supported by a Web Feature Service for the delivery of available data types (i.e. layers)

  • SERVICE=WFS & REQUEST=GetCapabilities

Retrieve a map image from a Web Map Service

  • SERVICE=WMS & REQUEST=GetMap

Retrieve data from an available coverage from a Web Coverage Service

  • SERVICE=WCS & REQUEST=GetCoverage
