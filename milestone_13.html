Milestone #13

Garrett Moise
5/12/2014


There have been many forest fires in the New Mexico area. I thought I'd address the problem of forest fires in the United States. Forest fires occur pver much of the Western and Southwestern United States and the hazards have been rising in recent years. The numbers of fires has increased and the readiness of firemen and the forest service has decreased. My map will show the topography of New Mexico, where the mountains are (where forest fires are more likely), where Emergency Dispatch Centers are, and where the fires have been burning lately.

This map will include overlays of forest fire areas, firestations, emergency outposts, and topographic features of New Mexico. Each overlay that I selected from the internet has a custom style that I created. These layers are hosted in my Geoserver stores. The WMS, which is the USGS Topo Basemap is hosted by the USGS website.
